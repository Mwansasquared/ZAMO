Our Objectives

  1. To advocate for inclusion.
  2. To educate to lessen stigma.
  3. To promote empowerment through skills.
  4. To promote the inclusion of persons with disabilities in relevant leadership, and mainstream government policies and programs.

Our Vision

To Educate, Inform and reach out.

Mission Statement

To strengthen the voice of albinism and vitiligo and to create a platform.
