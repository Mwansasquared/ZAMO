All Lives Matter

Every human being is important regardless of their race, skin color, gender, religion etc. It is therefore, important to understand and accept every diversity so as to co-exist in unity and harmony. Albinism is an inherited condition that affects the production of melanin, the pigment that colours the skin, hair and eyes. It is group of inherited disorders characterised by little or no melanin production. This condition increases the risk of skin cancer. Most people with albinism have pale skin, eye conditions and are sensitive to the sun. No cure exists, but skin can be protected and eye conditions can be treated.

Our Activities

The following are some of the activies we have undertaken:

Sunscreen distribution

Persons with albinism are vulnerable to the sun and have high risk of getting skin cancer. Therefore, as ZAMO, we have a Sunscreen distribution program and have managed to cover about 8 provinces where we currently distribute sunscreen. This is by far one of our biggest achievements.

School Program

One of our objectives as an organization is to see every child living with albinism and vitiligo attain an education. Through creating sustainable partnerships which culminate into sending the children that we sponsor to school, we solidify that vision one child a goal. So far, we have over 10 children in school.

Reading Clinic Library

Two years ago we successfully opened a reading clinic Library that offers skills and literacy to children, this helps us promote inclusiveness in the community and promote literacy and high performance in school.
